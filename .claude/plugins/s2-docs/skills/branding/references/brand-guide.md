## Logo Rules

### Available Versions

1. **Logo + claim** — "Anticipando un mundo ciberseguro" (default)
2. **Logo without claim** — When space is limited
3. **Symbol only** — Social media avatars, or when name is very close

### Safety Area & Minimum Sizes

- Safety area defined by the x-height of the R in the logotype
- With claim: min **3.5 cm** wide | Without claim: min **2 cm** wide
- At very small sizes, the symbol simplifies from 12 to 6 lines

### Co-branding

When placed alongside other brands, the x-height boxes regulate minimum distance between logos.

## Prohibited Logo Uses (8 rules)

1. Do not place the symbol above the text with the claim centered
2. Do not separate S2 from GRUPO
3. Do not separate symbol, S2, GRUPO, and claim by height
4. Do not rotate the logo (respect horizontality)
5. Do not remove the symbol
6. Do not deform proportions
7. Do not apply unauthorized effects (shadows, outlines, 3D)
8. Do not use on backgrounds that compromise legibility

## Prohibited Color Uses (8 rules)

1. Do not use lima on white backgrounds
2. Do not invent non-corporate colors
3. Do not use blue on black backgrounds
4. Do not use gradients as primary identity
5. Do not replace primary blue with gradient tones
6. Do not use semaphoric colors for branding
7. Do not mix gradients from different ranges
8. Do not use crudo as the only color without blue accent

## Writing Rule

Always write **S2GRUPO** — all uppercase, no space. Never "S2 Grupo", "s2grupo", or "S2-GRUPO".

**Claim:** "Anticipando un mundo ciberseguro"
